flathers 20150612

The xml files in this folder are 'typical' examples of their various standards.

eml_210.xml    			- Ecological Markup Language 2.1.0
esri.xml      			- The ArcGIS metadata format as of 10.0/10.1
fgdc.xml      			- Federal Geographic Data Committee Content Standard for
											Geospatial Metadata (with additional ESRI fields, ie
											thumbnail)
iso_19139.xml				- ISO 19115
iso_19115_2.xml			- ISO 19115-2
iso_19115_2_ds.xml	- ISO 19115-2 with DS_Series root element
dc_mdedit.xml				- A Dublin Core record created by the mdedit editor

They are not intended to be exhaustive in their expression of the standards
they represent.  They are here to serve as test metadata for the renderer.
They do not necessarily represent the dataset that they claim to; they may
have been altered from their original form in order to exhibit particular
metadata elements or characteristics.
